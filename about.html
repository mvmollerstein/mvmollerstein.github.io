I am Valentina Müller

I am a Visual Designer with a passion for slick designs and perfectly balanced color schemes. For the past 15 years I have been travelling the globe working in the fashion industry. Wether on the runway or behind the scenes - from Vogue to Garnier, or Malan Breton to Prada - in over 300 projects I have helped bring fantasies come to life. In the same time I completed my studies in educational sciences (with a focus on human-computer interaction) and psychological counselling. Ever since, I have worked at the intersection of Design, UX, Art and Fashion. Feel free to reach out. Whether we grab a cup of coffee or meet up online, I'm (almost) always up to discuss design trends, digital ideas or the top three lip syncs of RuPauls Drag Race. Click here to download my CV.
