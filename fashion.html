Artus

The dark, mysterious raven has been depicted in literature and legends through the ages. The Artus collection is inspired by the saga of Odin and puts focus on bold, dramatic, figure-hugging designs. Featuring heavy vegan leather, silk, and feather details.

Eve, its just another day

Eve combines femininity with romantic elements. Go-to chic, feminine pieces with unexpected silhouettes in nude shades. The combination of cotton, silk, cashmere, angora, and linen adds the perfect amount of comfort to a polished look.

Puppet

Must-have items for any true streetwear fan out there. Featuring constructions from velvet to vegan leather. Focusing on asymmetrical and oversized cuts - comfy yet crisp.
