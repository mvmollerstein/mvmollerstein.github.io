Urgrow

Urgrow is an innovative indoor garden that cares for itself and grows fresh, flavorful herbs, fruits, and vegetables. The app is a helpful guide on how to make the most of your harvest. Including the ability to snap and upload a picture of a plant – or pest – and having an expert gardener identify it for you. Consequently, you will receive alerts reminding you of your plant maintenance duties.

Smab

With Smab we created a human-centred approach, crafting an experience around everyday needs banking at your fingertips. Save and simple. Smab covers usual smart baking actions but also features to temporarily block and unblock your card, adjusting daily spending limits, storing account statements and documents, and helps you searching for ATMs nearby.
